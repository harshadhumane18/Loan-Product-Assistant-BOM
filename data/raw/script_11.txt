S.No. | Particulars | Scheme guidelines
Particulars: Name of the scheme
Scheme Guidelines: Loan against Property –for Individuals

Particulars: Purpose of loan
Scheme Guidelines: To cater to the requirements of meeting personal expenses.

Particulars: Eligibility
Scheme Guidelines: Permanent Employees of Central/ State Govt. /Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) (including Schools/ Colleges/ Educational Institutions), and Individual Businessmen, professionals and Self-employed individuals who own residential property/ commercial property /or building in his/her own name which are unencumbered and under self-occupation.

Particulars: Min Annual Income
Scheme Guidelines: Minimum annual Income – 5.00 Lakhs.

Particulars: Properties located at Metros
Scheme Guidelines: 10.00

Particulars: Margin
Scheme Guidelines: For Loans up to Rs.1.00 Cr:  40% of the Realizable value of the property  For Loans above Rs.1.00 Cr:  50% of the Realizable value of the property

Particulars: Repayment Period
Scheme Guidelines: Maximum Repayment period of 10 years or on borrower reaching the age of 75 years whichever is earlier.

Particulars: Rate of Interest
Scheme Guidelines: Click here to view the Interest Rate

Particulars: Deduction
Scheme Guidelines: Not to Exceed 60% of the Gross Income including Proposed EMI / Notional Interest.

Particulars: Security
Scheme Guidelines: Mortgage of the Property

Particulars: Processing Fee
Scheme Guidelines: 1 % of the loan amount + GST
