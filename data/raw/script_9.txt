--- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) ---
Q: What is a Personal Loan?
A: A Personal Loan is a loan that can be availed for any personal purpose such as home renovation, wedding, medical emergencies, travelling, debt repayments, bill payments and more. Unlike other loans, Personal Loan does not require any collateral or security and can be easily availed with minimum documentation. Like any other loan, you can repay Personal Loan with EMI (Equated Monthly Instalments); debited from your bank account.

Q: How does Personal Loan Work?
A: To avail the Personal Loan, all you need to do is submit the loan application form. On meeting the eligibility criteria, you will get an offer with the sanctioned amount, tenure of loan and interest rate. Once you accept the offer, funds are transferred to your bank account instantly.

Q: How can I know what my EMI amount is?
A: You can use the  Personal Loan EMI Calculator to know your EMI amount before applying for the Loan.

Q: What can a Personal Loan be used for?
A: Funds from Personal Loan can be used to cater to requirements of meeting personal expenses.
